An Intelligent Automatic Sea Forecasting System Targeting Specific Areas on Sailing Routes

针对航行路线特定区域的智能海洋预报系统

Abstract: Sailing vessel navigators always want to receive state-of-the-art prompt and accurate marine weather-forecasting services. However, the weather-routing services by private sectors are expensive. Further, forecasting results from public institutes are usually free, and they are not in real-time or numerical modes, so they are not quite suitable for small-size or offshore vessels. In this study, an intelligent system was constructed for delivering sea forecasting at specific areas according to the navigator’s order. The system can automatically obtain web-based forecasting charts issued from multi-source meteorological agencies and convert the regional information into numerical text at requested points. During this step, several intelligent algorithms, like the OpenCV digital image processing algorithm and the YOLO wind vector deep learning recognition method, were applied. By applying this state-of-the-art system, navigators on board do not need to download different institutional graphics (usually with large stream bytes) to explore the future states of the sea surface in a specific area in the sailing route but can obtain the multi-source text forecasting information just by sending the area coordinates to a designated email address. The field tests confirmed that this auto-intelligent system could assist the navigator within a few minutes and thus greatly enhance the navigation safety with minor text-based communication costs. It is expected that by improving the efficiency of marine services and bringing in more artificial intelligence technology, maritime security would be more sustainable.

摘要：航海员总是希望获得最及时准确的海洋天气预报。然而，私人部门的气象航线服务价格昂贵。此外，公共部门的预报通常是免费的，但它们不是实时的或采用数值模式呈现的，因此不太适合小型或近海船舶。这项研究构建了一个智能系统，根据航海员的指令在特定区域提供海洋天气预报。该系统可以自动获取来自多个气象机构发布的基于网络的预报图，并将区域信息在指定点转换为数值。在这个过程中，应用了一些智能算法，如 OpenCV 数字图像处理算法和 YOLO 风向量深度学习识别方法。通过这个系统，航海员不需要下载不同机构的图形（通常耗费大量网络流量或时间）来探索航行路线中特定区域的海面未来状态，而只需将区域坐标发送到指定的电子邮件地址即可获得多源文本预报信息。现场测试证实，这个自动智能系统可以在几分钟内为航海员提供帮助，从而以较小的基于文本通信的成本大大提高航行安全。通过提高海洋服务效率并引入更多人工智能技术，海上安全将具备更强稳定性。

Keywords: intelligent response system; sea forecasting; OpenCV image processing; YOLOv5 recognition

关键词：智能响应系统；海洋预报；OpenCV 图像处理；YOLOv5 识别

1. Introduction 引言

Weather elements, especially strong surface winds, are important factors to consider during marine navigation. Modern maritime transportation considers weather navigation as a necessity, and it can be adopted by three optimized modes: onshore weather navigation (referred to as shore navigation), ship-based weather navigation (referred to as self-navigation), and ship–shore integrated navigation [[1,2](##References 参考文献)]. Currently, onshore navigation has relatively mature navigation technology and working systems, while the level of automatic navigation is constantly improving [[3](##References 参考文献)]. Pure ship-based weather navigation relies greatly on the senior officers’ personal experiences and was adopted during the decades when ship–shore communication was slow and expensive; therefore, it is very hard to implement this navigation technique with reduced satellite communication costs. Ship–shore integrated navigation also offers many advantages, such as helping the captain to fully understand the intention of onshore alignment and maximizing cooperation with the shore navigation company to complete the navigation task. The use of automatic navigation can reduce the complicated communication and telegraph services of shore guidance and save navigation costs; it is an inevitable trend for future development.

天气因素，尤其是强表面风，是海上航行中需要考虑的重要因素。现代海上运输认为气象导航是必需的，它可以通过三种优化模式采集：岸上气象导航（称为岸上导航）、船载气象导航（称为自动导航）和船岸综合导航 [[1,2](##References 参考文献)]。目前，岸上导航具有相对成熟的导航技术和工作系统，而自动导航的水平还在不断提高 [[3](##References 参考文献)]。纯粹的船载气象导航在过往数十年中，因船岸通信缓慢且成本高昂，高度依赖高级船员个人经验；因此，在卫星通信成本降低的当下，很难实施这种导航技术。船岸综合导航也有许多优点，比如有助于船长理解岸上指令意图，最大程度与岸导公司协作完成航行任务。自动导航的运用可减少岸导繁杂的通信与电报服务环节，节约航行成本，是未来发展的必然趋向。

Large vessels typically followed climatology-based pre-set routes or applied commer cial meteorological navigation services (better known as weather routing, mostly solved by the A* algorithm) to receive a recommended route [[4-6](##References 参考文献)]. Weather-routing services are currently obligatory for transoceanic voyages, since providers prioritize navigation safety and economic purposes by considering various weather- and ship-related factors [[7,8](##References 参考文献)]. However, the recommended route may not always satisfy the captain’s preferences due to these competing considerations [[9](##References 参考文献)]. Moreover, weather-routing services are mostly expensive and have been dominated by a few private sectors. Most inland river, offshore, or short-distance voyages will not adopt weather-routing services, but this approach can pose a high risk if the ship encounters sudden severe weather conditions. For instance, the inland cruise ship, the “Oriental Star”, overturned in 2015 and claimed 442 lives [[10](##References 参考文献)]; this was the deadliest shipwreck accident in Chinese history since 1949. Even with advanced technology, deadly ship overturn accidents still happen. It was estimated that in 2020, 2021, and 2022, there were 24, 13, and 20 small ships that sunk or were overturned due to rough weather or rough sea conditions, claiming 140, 62, and 99 deaths or missing persons, respectively [[11-13](##References 参考文献)]. These facts show that sustainable weather navigation services are greatly needed.

大型船舶通常遵循基于气候学的预设航线，或采用商业气象导航服务（更常被称为气象航线，通常通过 A* 算法）推荐的航线 [[4-6](##References 参考文献)]。目前，跨洋航行必须使用气象航线服务，因为提供商通过考虑各种与天气和船舶相关的因素，优先考虑航行安全和经济目的 [[7,8](##References 参考文献)]。然而，由于这些相互竞争的考虑因素，推荐航线可能并不总是符合船长的偏好[[9](##References 参考文献)]。此外，气象航线服务大多价格昂贵，并且一直由少数私人部门主导。大多数内河、近海或短途航行不会采用气象航线服务，但如果船舶遇到突发的恶劣天气，这可能会带来很高的风险。例如，2015 年内陆游轮 “东方之星” 号倾覆，造成 442 人死亡 [[10](##References 参考文献)]; 这是自 1949 年以来中国历史上最致命的沉船事故。即使有先进的技术，致命的船舶倾覆事故仍然时有发生。据估计，在 2020 年、2021 年和 2022 年，分别有 24 艘、13 艘和 20 艘小型船舶因恶劣天气或海况恶劣而沉没或倾覆，分别造成 140 人、62 人和 99 人死亡或失踪 [[11-13](##References 参考文献)]。这些事实表明，可持续的气象导航服务是很有必要的。

In practice, small-size ships and particular types of offshore engineering vessels are vulnerable to sudden strong winds. The navigators in these types of vessels do not need a delicate and expensive transoceanic weather-routing service, nor a synoptic analysis from regional weather charts, but a 24/7, fast-responding sea condition prediction service at specific areas on sailing routes. Currently, these services are not available, at least in most offshore areas of the northwestern Pacific.

在实践中，小型船舶和特定类型的海洋工程船舶容易受到突如其来的强风的影响。这些类型船只的航海人员既不需要精细且昂贵的跨洋气象导航服务，也不需要来自区域天气图的天气分析，而是需要在航行路线的特定区域提供全天候、快速响应的海况预测服务。目前，这些服务至少在西北太平洋的大部分近海区域是不可用的。

In this study, on the basis of a simple model created ten years ago, an intensified and very complicated sustainable sea forecasting system was developed, aiming to fill the gap between basic self-navigation and expensive commercial weather-routing services, i.e., providing an innovative, real-time, and multi-source professional weather-consulting solution with almost no cost. The most innovative aspects of the new system are as follows: converting conventional forecasting graphics into digital formats through a deep learning image process, serving the navigator at the pixel level, and flexibly responding to multiple requests from navigators on board. After preliminary field tests, it has been proved that this concept is feasible.

在这项研究中，基于十年前创建的一个简单模型，开发了一个强化且非常复杂的可持续海洋预报系统，旨在填补基本的自导航和昂贵的商业气象航线服务之间的差距，即提供一种创新的、实时的、多源的专业天气咨询解决方案，且几乎没有成本。新系统最具创新性的方面如下：通过深度学习图像处理将传统的预报图形转换为数字格式，在像素级别为航海员提供服务，并灵活响应航海员的多个请求。经过初步的现场测试，已经证明这一概念是可行的。

2. Related Work and Methodology 相关工作和方法

Our approach contained two parts. First, we improved an auto-response pilot system
to retrieve multi-source prediction charts. Then, we applied several artificial intelligence
methods to this system to retrieve detailed prediction values at specific areas on sail-
ing routes.

我们的方法包括两个部分。首先，我们改进了一个自动响应试点系统，以检索多源预测图表。然后，我们将几种人工智能方法应用于该系统，以检索航线上特定区域的详细预测值。

2.1. Previous Auto-Response System 先前的自动响应系统

Currently, a modernvessel navigator can obtain manymarinewindandwaveforecasts from various sources. However, few of them are seaman-oriented, auto-responsive, and non-regional. In real-time marine wind and wave prediction methods, most designers have focused on producing state-of-the-art accurate forecasts and releasing them to the ship navigator at a routine frequency [[14](##References 参考文献)]. In many cases, the raw hydro-met forecasting data are retrieved from Numerical Weather Prediction (NWP) products, and statistical rendering or other mathematical algorithms like quantile-to-quantile and persistence methods are applied to improve the prediction accuracy [[15,16](##References 参考文献)]. Recently, Ou et al. [[17](##References 参考文献)] proposed a hybrid seasonal trend decomposition with loess (STL) decomposition deep neural network to perform efficient offshore wind speed forecasting. However, these forecasting systems there was an urgent need over the sea. were seldom user-oriented and often did not respond immediately when there was an urgent need over the sea.

当前，船舶导航员可从众多渠道获取大量海洋风浪预报信息。然而，其中罕有契合海员需求、具备自动响应能力且不受区域限制者。在实时海洋风浪预测方法领域，多数设计者专注于运用前沿技术作出精准预报，并依常规频率向导航员发布 [[14](##References 参考文献)]。很多情况下，原始水文气象预报数据采自数值天气预报（NWP）产品，且运用统计渲染或诸如Q-Q、连续性方法等数学算法提升预测精度 [[15,16](##References 参考文献)]。近期，Ou 等人 [[17](##References 参考文献)] 提出融合季节性趋势分解与黄土（STL）分解的深度神经网络，用以实现高效的海上风速预测。不过，此类预报系统往往存在不足，大多没有以用户实际需求为核心导向，在海上紧急状况突发急需时，难以即刻响应提供精准预报信息，致使船舶航行安全保障在关键节点面临风险，无法充分满足航海对气象预报实时性、精准性与灵活性的严苛要求。

Jian et al. [[18](##References 参考文献)] designed an automatic but non-intelligent forecasting system for sur face wind speed at marine fixed points. The core raw data were retrieved from the Euro pean Center for Medium Weather Forecasting (ECMWF) numerical product and sent to ships through the Beijing ground station server (Figure 1). Testing showed that it took 13 min (or 30 min) to receive a return email onboard by using an International Maritime Sat ellite organization (INMARST) F station (or C station). However, there were many factors beyond control that made the system unable to provide continuous service. One of the reasons is that too many servers were adopted in Europe, the USA, and China, and the connections between those servers were slow and unstable, resulting in long waiting times or even a failure to respond. In addition, it was difficult to acquire a long-term non-com mercial forecasting license from ECMWF.

Jian 等人 [[18](##References 参考文献)] 设计了一种针对海洋固定点海面风速的自动但非智能的预报系统。原始核心数据来自欧洲中期天气预报中心（ECMWF）的数值产品，并通过北京地面站服务器发送给船舶（图 1）。测试显示，使用国际海事卫星组织（INMARST）的 F 站（或 C 站）在船上接收回复电子邮件需要 13 分钟（或 30 分钟）。然而，有许多无法控制的因素导致该系统无法提供持续服务。原因之一是在欧洲、美国和中国采用了过多的服务器，并且这些服务器之间的连接缓慢且不稳定，导致长时间等待甚至无响应。此外，很难从 ECMWF 获得长期的非商业预报许可证。

To avoid the above shortcomings, hereby an innovative, comprehensive, and intelligent algorithm-incorporated system was re-built to combine all calculations and modules into a single server. By using various raw numerical model results or prediction charts issued by multiple institutions, we can obtain the latest forecasting, extract necessary weather-related information at specific points, and return them back to the ship navigator according to email-based requests. The entire process would be automatically executed within a few minutes. In general, the system significantly optimizes sea area positioning forecasts and reduces the error value of the weather forecast model, which helps vessel navigators prevent adverse weather events and ensure navigation safety and sustainability.

为规避上述缺陷，特此构建一个融合创新算法、全面且智能的系统，将所有计算与模块整合至单一服务器。凭借多家机构发布的各类原始数值模型结果或预报图表，我们能够获取最新预报，提取特定点必要的天气相关信息，并依据基于电子邮件的请求将其反馈给船舶导航员。整个流程依据电子邮件请求自动执行，且能在数分钟内完成。总体而言，该系统在数分钟内显著优化海域定位预报，降低气象预报模型误差值，帮助船舶导航员预防恶劣天气状况，确保航行安全与可持续性。

Besides ship users, this system can also be applied by offshore platforms in weather-sensitive work or tugging navigation. In the northern part of the South China Sea, the wind velocity observation showed an obvious linearity with the raw numerical forecasting value when the lead time was within 24 - 48 h (Figure 2). Based on this, a simple statistical linear regression correction was applied for offshore China.

除船舶外，该系统还可应用于海上平台的气象敏感作业或拖航导航。在南海北部，当预报时效处于 24 至 48 小时内时，风速观测值与原始数值预报值呈现明显的线性关系（图 2）。基于此，针对中国近海进行了简单的线性回归校正。

2.2. Pixel Reading through OpenCV Image Processing 通过OpenCV图像处理读取像素

Besides raw global dynamic model prediction, most official institutes issue wind and wave predictions in graphic mode free of charge. Therefore, one of the challenging problems is to convert the regional forecast results into a specific area forecast, which is what seamen want most. This study applied an artificial intelligence method, with OpenCV as the reading algorithm, to retrieve the wind speed and wave height from shaded charts at the pixel level.

除原始全球动态模型预报外，多数官方机构免费以图形模式发布风浪预报。因此，将区域预报结果转换为特定区域预报成为极具挑战性的难题之一，这也是船员最为迫切的需求。本研究运用一种人工智能方法，以 OpenCV 为读取算法，从阴影图表的像素层面检索风速与波高。

图 2. 三个海上平台风速观测值与欧洲中期天气预报中心（ECMWF）原始预报在 24 - 48 小时预报时效的散点图。2012 年 3 月至 11 月期间每天收集两次超过 500 个风速数据点。

OpenCV is a widely used open-source computer vision library that provides many functions and tools for image and video processing and can be used in many programming languages such as C++, Python, and Java. OpenCV can help developers solve various computer vision problems, including object detection, face recognition, image segmentation, motion tracking, and more. OpenCV was developed by Intel in 1999 and has now become the foundation of many computer vision applications [[19](##References 参考文献)]. In this study, it was feasible to apply OpenCV to retrieve the pixel color at the sailing route point and convert it into wind scale or wave heights, since most of the online forecasting graphics are in color shade mode.

OpenCV 是一个广泛使用的开源计算机视觉库，它为图像和视频处理提供众多功能与工具，并且可用于 C++、Python 和 Java 等多种编程语言。OpenCV 能够协助开发者解决各类计算机视觉问题，诸如目标检测、人脸识别、图像分割、运动跟踪等等。OpenCV 由英特尔于 1999 年开发，如今已成为众多计算机视觉应用的基石 [[19](##References 参考文献)]。在本研究中，由于多数在线预报图形采用色彩阴影模式，所以运用 OpenCV 检索航行路线点的像素颜色，并将其转换为风级或浪高是切实可行的。

2.3. Wind Vector Recognition by YOLO Deep Learning Algorithm 基于 YOLO 深度学习算法的风矢量识别

With the rapid application and expansion of deep learning in computer vision, related target detection methods have gradually replaced the traditional handcrafted feature design and become a research hotspot in various industries. The early target detection algorithms relied heavily on manual feature extraction, traversed the image through sliding windows, and finally determined the target class by classifiers. However, these methods have some limitations, such as high computational time, slow speed, high error rate, and strong subjectivity [[20](##References 参考文献)]. In contrast, object detection algorithms based on deep learning offer high operational efficiency, fast processing speed, and superior detection accuracy, making them suitable for real-time detection. The use of computer vision and convolutional neural network (CNN) has become the dominant approach for multi-purpose target detections. Deep learning target detection algorithms can be divided into one-stage and two-stage. Two-stage algorithms first generate a region proposal and then subject it to CNN for target classification and position regression. Typical models include R-CNN [[21](##References 参考文献)], Fast R-CNN [[22](##References 参考文献)], and Faster R-CNN [[23](##References 参考文献)]. This method achieves high detection accuracy but with relatively slow speed, so it is not investigated in this study. In contrast, the one-stage algorithm directly classifies and predicts the position of targets without generating region boxes, represented by SSD [[24](##References 参考文献)], the YOLO series [[25](##References 参考文献)], etc. It provides a fast detection speed but with reduced accuracy [[26](##References 参考文献)]. It should be noted that since its first release in 2015, the YOLO-Based Deep Learning Models for Object Detection have been updated rapidly, especially in recent years. For instance, YOLOv3, v4, v5, v6, v7, and v8 were released in April 2018, April 2020, May 2020, June 2022, July 2022, and January 2023, from four different research groups. When the study began in 2021, the latest YOLO models, i.e., YOLOv4 and YOLOv5, were applied to detect wind vectors from Japanese 24 h and 48 h surface weather forecast charts, reflecting the regional marine wind scales. YOLOv7 and YOLOv8, released recently, focused on the aspects of pedestrians, damaged racks, and fracture detection [[27](##References 参考文献)].

随着深度学习在计算机视觉领域的快速应用与拓展，相关目标检测方法逐渐取代传统手工特征设计，成为各行业研究热点。早期目标检测算法严重依赖手工特征提取，通过滑动窗口遍历图像，最后由分类器确定目标类别。然而，这些方法存在局限性，如计算耗时久、速度慢、错误率高、主观性强 [[20](##References 参考文献)]。相比之下，基于深度学习的目标检测算法运算效率高、处理速度快、检测精度优，适用于实时检测。计算机视觉与卷积神经网络（CNN）的结合已成为多用途目标检测的主流方式。深度学习目标检测算法可分一阶段和两阶段。二阶段算法首先生成区域提议，再经 CNN 进行目标分类与位置回归，典型模型有 R-CNN [[21](##References 参考文献)]、Fast R-CNN [[22](##References 参考文献)]、Faster R-CNN [[23](##References 参考文献)]，此方法精度高但速度慢，故本研究未涉。一阶段算法则直接分类预测目标位置，不生成区域框，以 SSD [[24](##References 参考文献)]、YOLO 系列 [[25](##References 参考文献)] 为代表，检测速度快但精度略降 [[26](##References 参考文献)]。需注意，自 2015 年首次发布，基于 YOLO 的深度学习目标检测模型更新迅速，近年尤甚。如 2018 年 4 月、2020 年 4 月、2020 年 5 月、2022 年 6 月、2022 年 7 月、2023 年 1 月，四个不同研究团队分别发布 YOLOv3、v4、v5、v6、v7、v8。2021 年研究伊始，最新的 YOLOv4 和 YOLOv5 被用于检测日本 24 小时和 48 小时地面天气预报图中风矢量，以反映区域海洋风级。近期发布的 YOLOv7 和 YOLOv8 聚焦于行人、受损货架及骨折检测 [[27](##References 参考文献)]。

2.3.1. YOLO4

YOLOv4 is a deep learning-based target detection algorithm that achieves recognition of objects in an image by analyzing the image in multiple stages using a convolutional neural network.

YOLOv4 是一种基于深度学习的目标检测算法，通过卷积神经网络对图像进行多阶段分析以实现图像中目标的识别。

Stage 1: Feature Extraction.YOLOv4 uses convolutional neural network (CNN) to extract the features of the image. CNN does this by continuously convolving, pooling, and non-linear activation of the image to abstract the most important features in the image [[28](##References 参考文献)].

阶段 1：特征提取。YOLOv4 运用卷积神经网络（CNN）提取图像特征。CNN 通过对图像持续进行卷积、池化和非线性激活操作，抽象出图像中最重要的特征 [[28](##References 参考文献)]。

Phase 2: Image Pyramid. In order to cover objects of different sizes, YOLOv4 uses the image pyramid technique, where the image is sampled at multiple resolutions. This allows the model to recognize both small and large objects.

阶段 2：图像金字塔。为覆盖不同尺寸的目标，YOLOv4 采用图像金字塔技术，以多种分辨率对图像采样。这使模型能识别不同大小的目标。

Phase 3: Object Detection.YOLOv4 uses a technique called Multi-Channel Prediction (MCP), which performs object detection through different prediction layers. Each layer corresponds to a different resolution to recognize objects of different sizes.

阶段 3：目标检测。YOLOv4 运用一种名为多通道预测（MCP）的技术，经不同预测层执行目标检测。每层对应不同分辨率，以识别不同尺寸的目标。

Phase 4: Object Recognition.YOLOv4 uses a Softmax classifier to recognize objects in an image by assigning each object to a predefined category.

阶段 4：目标识别。YOLOv4 采用 Softmax 分类器，通过将每个目标分配到预定义类别来识别图像中的目标。

YOLOv4 does this by dividing the image into multiple grids of different sizes and then using a convolutional neural network to detect the presence of an object within each grid and the location of the object. Each grid detects multiple different boxes, and each box predicts the class and confidence of the object it contains [[29](##References 参考文献)]. YOLOv4 improves on previous versions of YOLO to increase accuracy and speed. It uses more convolutional layers and uses more data augmentation techniques to train the model to improve its ability to recognize complex images. During the training process, the YOLOv4 model is validated by continuously modifying the network weights to minimize the prediction error and by cross-validation to verify the accuracy of the model. Finally, the model will predict the bounding box of the target on the test data and the accuracy of the model is evaluated using the IoU of the predicted bounding box versus the true bounding box.

YOLOv4 通过将图像划分为不同尺寸的多个网格，然后利用卷积神经网络检测每个网格内目标的存在及位置。每个网格检测多个不同框体，每个框体预测其所包含目标的类别与置信度 [[29](##References 参考文献)]。YOLOv4 对之前的 YOLO 版本加以改进，提升了精度与速度。它采用更多卷积层并运用更多数据增强技术训练模型，增强其识别复杂图像的能力。在训练过程中，通过不断修改网络权重使预测误差最小化来验证 YOLOv4 模型，并通过交叉验证核实模型精度。最后，模型将在测试数据上预测目标的边界框，并依据预测边界框与真实边界框的交并比（IoU）评估模型精度。

2.3.2. YOLOv5

The model of YOLOv5consists of three parts: a feature extraction network, a feature pooling layer, and a multitask prediction layer. The feature extraction network consists of a series of convolutional layers used to extract features from the original image. Connecting multiple feature extraction layers into a fully connected network allows more features to be extracted, making the model more effective in extracting useful information. The feature pooling layer is used to pool features from the original input image and map the features to a specific spatial scale so that the multitask prediction layer can better handle the input features. Finally, the multitask prediction layer converts the features into predictions of object categories and detection frames for object detection purposes. The model principle of YOLOv5 is based on feature extraction from images, where the objects in the image are extracted and used as inputs to the model [[30](##References 参考文献)]. YOLOv5 uses convolutional neural networks (CNNs) for feature extraction, where the network structure of the convolutional neural network uses a multi-layer network consisting of a feed-forward network and a feedback network consisting of multiple layers, each with a different convolutional kernel and pooling layer. It builds a three-layer deep neural network that receives features from the output of the feature extraction module and uses these features to predict the target’s localization information. It is implemented through a special multi-task learning framework where each task has a separate output layer to predict the output of each localization task.

YOLOv5 模型由三部分构成：特征提取网络、特征池化层以及多任务预测层。特征提取网络包含一系列卷积层，用于从原始图像中提取特征。将多个特征提取层连接为全连接网络，从而提取更多特征，使模型能更有效地提取有用信息。特征池化层用于对原始输入图像的特征进行池化处理，并将特征映射到特定空间尺度，以便多任务预测层能更好地处理输入特征。最后，多任务预测层将特征转化为目标类别预测和检测框，用于目标检测。YOLOv5 的模型原理基于图像特征提取，即提取图像中的目标作为模型输入 [[30](##References 参考文献)]。YOLOv5 运用卷积神经网络（CNNs）进行特征提取，其中卷积神经网络的网络结构采用由前馈网络和反馈网络组成的多层网络，每层具有不同的卷积核和池化层。它构建了一个三层深度神经网络，接收来自特征提取模块输出的特征，并利用这些特征预测目标的定位信息。这是通过一种特殊的多任务学习框架实现的，其中每个任务都有单独的输出层来预测每个定位任务的输出。

When the input image enters the network, the model decomposes the image into small chunks and uses a specialized convolutional kernel to convolve each chunk of the image, and then passes through a pooling layer where the convolved features are aggregated to extract features from the image. The output features of each layer are passed on to the next layer, then the model can extract features from the image in depth, thus obtaining more accurate object detection results.

当输入图像进入网络时，模型将图像分解为小块，并使用专门的卷积核对图像的每一块进行卷积，然后经过池化层，在池化层中卷积特征被聚合以从图像中提取特征。每层的输出特征被传递到下一层，接着模型能够深度地从图像中提取特征，从而获得更精确的目标检测结果。

After the network training is completed, the model splits the image into several chunks and detects each chunk to determine whether it contains an object. When an object is detected, the model calculates the bounding box of the object and gives the class and confidence level of the object.

网络训练完成后，模型将图像分割为若干小块并对每块进行检测，以判定其是否包含目标。当检测到目标时，模型计算目标的边界框，并给出目标的类别与置信水平。

2.3.3. YOLOv5s

YOLOv5s is the smallest and fastest version of the YOLOv5 algorithm. Due to the advantages in both size and computation speed, it was chosen in our experiment. The specific structure diagram is shown in Figure 3. The backbone network is composed of Focus structure and CSP structure, mainly used to extract target features [[31](##References 参考文献)]. To prevent information loss during down-sampling, the model uses letterboxing to adaptively resize the 2392 × 2048 × 3 image to a 640 × 640 × 3 image. The Focus module then converts the resized image into a 320 × 320 × 32 feature map, by performing a slice operation on the image. In contrast to a regular CBL, the CSP structure [[32](##References 参考文献)] divides the original input into two branches, which are processed separately with convolutional operations to reduce the number of channels. One of the branches then undergoes a Bottleneck × N operation, and the two branches are subsequently merged to ensure that the input and output of the Bottleneck CSP are of equal size. This enables the model to obtain richer feature information through local cross-layer fusion while reducing computational complexity. The CSP1_X and CSP2_X structures shown in [Figure 3](######Figure 3. YOLOv5s frame in wind vector recognition.) illustrate this approach. The SPP module in YOLOv5s borrows the idea of SPPNet, which uses convolutional kernels with uniform stride but different sizes to implement SPP. A uniform stride means that the output feature map size is the same, and only the sensitivity to different regions varies. After concatenation, a 1 ×1convolution is used to fuse the features. During the object detection process, the model extracts multiple feature layers and a total of three feature layers are extracted. Each feature layer divides the entire image into small grids,with each grid point responsible for detecting an area. Finally, predictions are made based on the feature results, resulting in three prediction maps with sizes of 80 × 80 × 255, 40 × 40 × 255, and 20 × 20 × 255.

YOLOv5s 是 YOLOv5 算法中规模最小且速度最快的版本。鉴于其在规模与计算速度方面的优势，我们选用了它作为实验模型。其具体结构示意图见图 3。骨干网络由 Focus 结构和 CSP 结构构成，主要用于提取目标特征 [[31](##References 参考文献)]。为防止下采样期间的信息丢失，模型采用 letterboxing 方法将 2392×2048×3 的图像自适应调整为 640×640×3 的图像。随后，Focus 模块通过对图像执行切片操作，将调整大小后的图像转换为 320×320×32 的特征图。与常规的 CBL 不同，CSP 结构 [[32](##References 参考文献)] 将原始输入划分为两个分支，分别经卷积运算处理以减少通道数量。其中一个分支随后进行 Bottleneck×N 操作，接着将两个分支合并，确保 Bottleneck CSP 的输入与输出尺寸相等。这使模型能够通过局部跨层融合获取更丰富的特征信息，同时降低计算复杂度。图 3 所示的 CSP1_X 和 CSP2_X 结构阐释了此方法。YOLOv5s 中的 SPP 模块借鉴了 SPPNet 的理念，即采用步幅一致但尺寸各异的卷积核实现 SPP。一致的步幅意味着输出特征图尺寸相同，仅对不同区域的敏感度存在差异。拼接后，利用 1×1 卷积融合特征。在目标检测过程中，模型提取多个特征层，共提取三个特征层。每个特征层将整幅图像划分为小网格，每个网格点负责检测一个区域。最后，依据特征结果进行预测，生成尺寸分别为 80×80×255、40×40×255 和 20×20×255 的三个预测图。

4. Graphic Forecasting Data Retrieval 图形预测数据检索

4.1. Classification and Storage of Weather Forecast 天气预报的分类与存储

To obtain the correct weather forecast map, the system initially performs a simple classification of the image based on various attributes. These include binary byte, tuple, file type, color, brightness, and resolution, among others. For instance, when retrieving pictures from the China National Meteorological Center, we eliminate any unnecessary ones with binary bytes greater than 100,000. Subsequently, the remaining pictures undergo classification according to different tuples. A tuple with [7937953] represents a wind forecast map, a tuple with [6988603] indicates a sea fog forecast map, and a tuple with [7168603] represents a strong convection forecast map. Finally, the pictures are sorted based on time and saved in predetermined folders.

为获取准确的天气预报图，系统首先依据多种属性对图像进行简单分类。这些属性包括二进制字节、元组、文件类型、颜色、亮度及分辨率等。例如，从中国国家气象中心检索图片时，我们剔除二进制字节大于 10 万的任何不必要图片。随后，剩余图片依不同元组分类。元组为 [7937953] 代表风预报图，元组 [6988603] 表示海雾预报图，元组 [7168603] 代表强对流预报图。最后，图片依时间排序并保存于预定文件夹。

For weather forecast maps that are too similar to be classified based on image attributes, a grid-based Kmeans algorithm was adopted [[35](##References 参考文献)]. The traditional Kmeans algorithm needs to go through the whole dataset. The grid-based Kmeans algorithm is an improved Kmeans clustering algorithm. It reduces computational complexity by dividing the dataset into grids, reducing the memory usage, and thereby improving the efficiency of the algorithm. The maps are first standardized and cropped to eliminate any abnormalities. Next, K centers are randomly selected, and each image is assigned to the nearest center. These cluster centers then compete with each other to adjust their positions so that their distance from the maximum possible data is less than the distance from other cluster centers. The center of each category is recalculated after this competition. This process is repeated until the change in each type of center triggers a pre-set threshold, thus completing the picture classification.

对于因过于相似而无法基于图像属性分类的天气预报图，采用基于网格的 Kmeans 算法 [[35](##References 参考文献)]。传统 Kmeans 算法需遍历整个数据集。基于网格的 Kmeans 算法是改进的 Kmeans 聚类算法，通过将数据集划分为网格降低计算复杂度、减少内存使用量，进而提升算法效率。首先对地图进行标准化与裁剪以消除异常。接着随机选取 K 个中心，将每张图像分配至最近中心。然后这些聚类中心相互竞争调整位置，使其与尽可能多的数据距离小于与其他聚类中心的距离。在此竞争后重新计算各类别的中心。重复此过程直至各类别中心的变化触发预设阈值，从而完成图片分类。

7. Conclusions and Future Research Discussion 结论和未来的研究讨论

7.1. Conclusions: An Automatic System with Artificial Intelligence 结论：一个具备人工智能的自动化系统

With advanced artificial intelligence methods, a real-time automatic response system was built to provide marine wind and wave forecasts in specific areas per request from the sailing ship. The system is capable of running at no cost and giving rapid response in just a few minutes. So far, this system only focuses on the northwest Pacific Ocean for wind and wave prediction, but it can be easily extended to more marine areas and institutional predictions, so a broad sustainable application is expected. In addition, there are also some shortcomings, such as hard language and the inability to achieve route planning.

借助人工智能，构建了一个实时自动响应系统，该系统能够响应航行船舶的请求，针对特定区域提供海洋风浪预报。此系统能够没有成本情况下运行，并能在短短几分钟内迅速作出响应。到目前为止，该系统仅聚焦于西北太平洋区域的风浪预测，但它可以轻松扩展至更多海洋区域，因此有望获得广泛应用。此外，该系统也存在一些不足之处，比如语言生硬以及无法实现航线规划。

Based on a numerical weather product and natural language method, Jian et al. [[41](##References 参考文献)] carried out a parrel study to convert the raw digital 7-day wind speed forecasts into a natural language description. However, it is not applied to the current forecasting system due to a lack of adequate time series data. In the future, a comprehensive meteorological language database would be expected to provide more humanized meteorological information services by implementing more artificial intelligence methods.

基于数值天气产品和自然语言方法，Jian等人 [[41](##References 参考文献)] 开展了一项并行研究，旨在将原始的为期 7 天的数字风速预报转换为自然语言描述。然而，由于缺乏足够的时间序列数据，这一方法尚未应用于当前的预报系统中。未来，有望通过运用更多人工智能方法构建一个综合性的气象语言数据库，从而提供更人性化的气象信息服务。

7.2. Future Research: Decision Problem with More Artificial Intelligence Algorithms 未来研究：运用更多人工智能算法解决决策问题

This study was performed under the assumption of letting the navigators on board to choose their preferred institutional forecasts, thus skipping the decision problem. In the future, advanced optimization algorithms (e.g., hybrid heuristics, metaheuristics, hyper heuristics, adaptive algorithms, self-adaptive algorithms, island algorithms, polyploid algorithms) should be applied for challenging decision problems [[42 - 47](##References 参考文献)].

本研究是在假定让船上航行人员自行选择他们偏好的机构预报的情况下开展的，因而跳过了决策问题。未来应当将先进的优化算法（例如，混合启发式算法、元启发式算法、超启发式算法、自适应算法、自自适应算法、孤岛算法、多倍体算法）应用于具有挑战性的决策问题 [[42 - 47](##References 参考文献)]。

Heuristics algorithms, or simple heuristics algorithms, usually adapt to the current problem and try to fully utilize the particularity of this problem without reaching the global optimal solution. Due to their excessive greed, they often fall into a local optimal state. However, due to their efficiency and resource saving, they have already been widely applied in nautical weather routing problems, e.g., A* and greedy algorithm.

启发式算法，或简单启发式算法，通常会适应当前问题，试图充分利用该问题的特殊性而无法达到全局最优解。由于它们过于 “贪婪”，常常会陷入局部最优状态。不过，因其高效且节省资源的特点，它们已经在航海气象航线规划问题中得到了广泛应用，例如 A * 算法和贪心算法。

Metaheuristic algorithms are problem-independent techniques. Generally speaking, they are not greedy. In fact, they may even accept temporary deterioration of the solution in a specific problem. Metaheuristic algorithms find optimal solutions by modeling natural phenomena(e.g., heredity, ants looking for food, etc.), so they can be considered to simulate the marine navigators looking for a suitable route.

元启发式算法属于与具体问题无特定关联的技术手段。通常而言，这类算法并不具有 “贪婪性”。实际上，在特定的问题情境中，它们甚至能够接纳解的阶段性恶化状况。元启发式算法借助对自然现象（如遗传现象、蚂蚁觅食行为等）的模拟建模来探寻最优解，从这个角度看，其可被视作对海上航行人员寻觅适宜航线过程的一种模拟呈现。

Hyper-heuristics find better solutions for special problems, while metaheuristics do not add any special conditions to find the general solution space for general problems. The particularity of hyper-heuristic algorithms lies in the fact that the space they find is not the solution space, but the heuristic or metaheuristic space.

超启发式算法侧重于为特殊问题寻觅更优解答，而元启发式算法则不会额外附加特殊条件去定位一般问题的通用解空间范围。超启发式算法的特殊之处在于，其探寻的并非直接的解空间，而是启发式算法空间或者元启发式算法空间。

There are many different domains where advanced optimization algorithms have been applied as solution approaches, such as online learning, scheduling, multi-objective optimization, transportation, medicine, data classification, and others. The proposed approach could be compared to various advanced optimization algorithms.

当前，先进的优化算法已在众多不同领域得到应用，成为各类问题的解决途径，例如在线学习、任务调度安排、多目标优化、交通运输、医学以及数据分类等等。本研究提出的方法可与多种先进的优化算法展开对比分析，从而进一步凸显其特性与优势，为相关领域的研究与实践提供更多参考与借鉴。
